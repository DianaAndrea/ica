About the DJ

Diana
Perez

Born and raised amidst the sunny vibes of California, ICA — Ice Cream Adventure — has honed her craft to perfection, infusing her sets with infectious beats and a touch of West Coast flavor.

Whether she's spinning in cozy Salinas venues or holding it down at Monterey First Sundays for house enthusiasts, ICA brings an energy that's equal parts warm, adventurous, and undeniably groovy.

Her mission: "On a most excellent adventure to create and innovate within my realm of creation." Find her sets on Mixcloud, her journey on Instagram, and her bookings in your inbox. ✨
